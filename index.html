We start with your contract period and work backwards:

  1. Weekends removed — only Monday to Friday counts
  2. UK bank holidays removed — these are excluded automatically
  3. Your weekly pattern applied — if you work 3 days a week, we take 3 days from each available working week
  4. Planned leave deducted — any leave you enter is taken off the days you're actually scheduled to work

So if you work 3 days/week and take 3 days leave, those 3 leave days come from your working days — not from weekends or bank holidays.
